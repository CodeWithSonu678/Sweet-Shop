About Us

Mithai Ghar mein aapka swagat hai, jahan har ek mithai mein milti hai pyar aur traditional swaad. Humare yahaan aapko milegi har tarah ki mithai, jaise ki peda, laddu, barfi, jalebi, gulab jamun, aur bohot kuch. Hamari mithaiyan 100% organic aur fresh ingredients se banti hain, jo har bite mein apne asli swaad ka ehsaas dilati hain. Chhote ya bade tyohar, ya bas apne liye thodi si meethaai chahiye ho, humare dukan par aapko milenge har occasion ke liye perfect mithaiyan.
